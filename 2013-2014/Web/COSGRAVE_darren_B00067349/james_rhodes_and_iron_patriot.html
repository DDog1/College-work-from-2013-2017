Colonel James Rhodes / Iron Patriot

Played by Don Cheadle.

A colonel in the U.S. airforce and is a good friend of Tony Stark.
Just like Stark he has his own high tech battle suit called the Iron Patriot armor.
The armor gives him strength, durability, flight and weaponry. He sometimes helps
Tony Stark, by giving him Intel on bad guys or he would help him
in the fights that Tony can't handle on his own.
