Storyline

Tony stark is a billionare and brilliant industrialist. He is captures by terriorists who want him to build a weapon.
To escape he has to build a suit of armour. When back home he improves the armour and becomes the super hero Iron Man.
In Iron Man 3 he is confronted by an enemy who knows no bounds, The Mandarin, the leader of a terriorist organisation.
The Mandarin wants to seek revenge for a past humiliation. Iron Man challenges The Mandarin which leads to a chain of
events which leaves Iron Man homeless, on the run and leaves his Girlfriend suffering from the Extremis virus,
which causes the victim to explode. Iron Man finds out that this is the scource of several global terrorist attacks.
With his back against the wall, Stark is left to survive on his own, relying on his armor and wits. As he fights
his way through bad guys, Stark discovers the answer to the question that has secretly haunted him:
Does the man make the suit or does the suit make the man?
