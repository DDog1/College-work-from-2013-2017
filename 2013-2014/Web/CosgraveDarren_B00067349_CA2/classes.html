Yoga

a Hindu spiritual and ascetic discipline,
a part of which, including breath control,
simple meditation, and the adoption of specific
bodily postures, is widely practised for health
and relaxation.

Pilates

a system of exercises using special apparatus,
designed to improve physical strength, flexibility,
and posture, and enhance mental awareness.
